# Research Assistant

An agent that turns a question into a researched, cited answer: it plans
searches, reads the best sources, and synthesizes findings.

## What it does

- Breaks a question into sub-queries
- Gathers and skims sources, keeping the useful ones
- Writes a concise summary with links back to sources

## Prompt sketch

> You are a research assistant. Given a question, search broadly, verify claims
> against multiple sources, and produce a short, cited synthesis.
