# Why a command bar?

Most sites bury navigation in menus. This one puts a Claude-Code-style prompt at
the bottom of every page: type `/` and go anywhere.

It's not just an aesthetic. A flat, command-driven surface plus structured JSON
manifests means an agent can traverse the whole site the same way a person does —
by intent, not by hunting through markup.

Two doors, one structure: the command bar for people, `index.json` for agents.
